What are Muscle coins?

Muscle coins are the community decentralized currency of PullApp - look at the global leaderboard of the highest earners to discover your rank.

How do I earn Muscle coins?

You can gain Muscle coins by:

  • Completing, Judging or creating Challenges
  • Founding and contributing to new spots
